  • When It All Began...

    "Dinosaur to Inventor"

    I'm the yellow one! A tomboy, an identical twin, and a ball of energy. I said "dinosaur" when my kindergarten teacher first asked me what I wanted to be when I grew up...on my second try, I chose "inventor". From an early age I think I always knew I was going to be an engineer :)

  • 2005 - 2009

    Electrical Engineering (BSc)

    Cornell University

    Within the first month of being at Cornell, I got my first real taste in engineering when I joined Cornell's Formula SAE racing team. During my two years on the team I worked with 2 others to design our car's dashboard and wiring harness. Joining FSAE was one of the best decisions I have ever made and has really helped shape my entire engineering career.

  • 2009 - 2012

    Engineering Leadership Developement Program

    Lockheed Martin

    Upon graduating, I was selected to join Lockheed Martin's Engineering Leadership Development Program (ELDP). During this rotational program, I did everything from design a tunnel-detection radar to venture capital for Lockheed's advanced research and development organization. ELDP has taught me never be afraid to pursue opportunities - no matter how far off from reality they may seem.

  • 2009 - 2012

    Systems Engineering (MEng)

    Cornell University

    While working full time at Lockheed Martin, I earned my Master's in System's Engineering - which I later learned was really a degree in product management and systems architecture. My systems degree really helped me form a strong set of design principles and taught me how to turn a concept into a product.

  • 2012 - Present

    Building Navy Simulations

    Lockheed Martin

    Three years of rotations in ELDP led me to a really interesting rapid prototyping group within Lockheed, where I've primarily built prototype software applications for the US Navy. MissionXplorer, my most recent application, has won multiple company recognition awards and is starting to make big waves in domestic and international Navy communities around the world.
